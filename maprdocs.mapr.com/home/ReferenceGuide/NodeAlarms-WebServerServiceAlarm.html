WebServer Service Alarm UI Column Webserver Alarm Logged As NODE_ALARM_SERVICE_WEBSERVER_DOWN Meaning The WebServer service on the node has stopped running. Resolution Go to the [1]node information page or the Services page in MCS to check whether the WebServer service is running. The warden will try three times to restart the service automatically. After an interval (30 minutes by default) the warden will again try three times to restart the service. The interval can be configured using the parameter services.retryinterval.time.sec in warden.conf. If the warden successfully restarts the WebServer service, the alarm is cleared. If the warden is unable to restart the WebServer service, it may be necessary to contact technical support.
