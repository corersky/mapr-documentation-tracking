Elasticsearch Formatting Alarm Logged As NODE_ALARM_SERVICE_ELASTICSEARCH_EXCP Meaning The put of primary table cannot be converted/formatted for pushing to ElasticSearch. If the primary table has ElasticSearch as one of its replicas, that may miss updates on some rows as they could not be converted to a supported format for ElasticSearch. Resolution Check for rows that could not be formatted for pushing to ElasticSearch in MFS logs and insert row with data that can be pushed to ElasticSearch.
