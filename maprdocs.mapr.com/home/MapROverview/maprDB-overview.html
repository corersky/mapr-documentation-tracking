MapR-DB MapR-DB is an enterprise-grade, high-performance, NoSQL database management system. You can use it for real-time, operational analytics capabilities. Why MapR-DB? MapR-DB is built into the MapR platform. It requires no additional process to manage, leverages the same architecture as the rest of the platform, and requires minimal additional management.
