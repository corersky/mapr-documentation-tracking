Configuring Hive Metastore Clients to use MapR-SASL The Hive metastore clients are configured to use MapR-SASL when authenticating with Hive Metastore. Note: Hive Metastore clients must provide a valid MapR ticket to connect to the Hive Metastore. See [1]Connecting to Hive for details. 1. Ensure that the cluster is secure. 2.
