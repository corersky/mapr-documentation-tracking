Setting the Refresh Rate in the MapR Control System You can configure how frequently you want to refresh the graphs and data in the panes. To set refresh rate: 1. Log in to MCS and click [colRefresh.png] to display the Refresh Rate window. 2. Log in to MCS and click Settings from the [userIcon.png] dropdown menu. 3. Enter the refresh rate in seconds. In v6.0, the minimum refresh interval rate is 5 seconds and by default, graphs are refreshed every 15 seconds. In v6.0.1, the minimum and default refresh interval rate is 30 seconds. 4. Click Save Changes for the changes to take effect.
