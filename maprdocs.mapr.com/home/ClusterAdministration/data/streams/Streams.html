Administering Streams MapR-ES brings integrated publish and subscribe messaging to the MapR Converged Data Platform. Producer applications can publish messages to topics, which are logical collections of messages, and Consumer applications can read those messages at their own pace. Topics are grouped into streams, for which administrators can apply security, retention, and replication policies. [1][USEMAP:streams-trilogy.png] References 1.
