Check the Status of a Job You can check the status of a job from the command line or the Oozie Web Console. From the command line, issue the following (substituting the job ID for the placeholder): # /opt/mapr/oozie/oozie-/bin/oozie job -info To check status from the Oozie Web Console, point your browser to http://:/oozie and click All Jobs.
