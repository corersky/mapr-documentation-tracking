NFSv4 Service Alarm UI Column NFSv4 Service Down Logged As NODE_ALARM_SERVICE_NFS4_DOWN Meaning The NFSv4 service on the node has stopped running. Resolution Go to the [1]node information page or the Services page in MCS to check whether the NFS service is running. The warden will try three times to restart the service automatically. After an interval (30 minutes by default) the warden will again try three times to restart the service. The interval can be configured using the parameter services.retryinterval.time.sec in [2]warden.conf file. If the warden successfully restarts the NFS service, the alarm is cleared. If the warden is unable to restart the NFS service, refer to [3]NFSv4 Troubleshooting to restart the service.
