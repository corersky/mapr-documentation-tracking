Configuring Zeppelin Interpreters Out-of-box, the interpreters in Apache Zeppelin on MapR are preconfigured to run against different backend engines. You may need to perform manual steps to configure the Livy, Spark, and JDBC interpreters. No additional steps are needed to configure and run the Pig and Shell interpreters. You can configure the idle timeout threshold for interpreters. To configure an interpreter, click on your login name in the top right corner of the Zeppelin home screen and select Interpreter: [Interpreters.png]
