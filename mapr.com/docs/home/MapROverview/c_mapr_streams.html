MapR-ES MapR-ES brings integrated publish and subscribe messaging to the MapR Converged Data Platform. Why MapR-ES? MapR-ES is built into the MapR platform. It requires no additional process to manage, leverages the same architecture as the rest of the platform, and requires minimal additional management.
