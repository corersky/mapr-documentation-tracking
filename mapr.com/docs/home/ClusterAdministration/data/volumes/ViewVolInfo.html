Viewing Volume Information You can retrieve and view volume information using MCS and the CLI. 1. Log in to MCS and go to the Summary tab under Data > Volumes. 2. Click the volume name in the Volumes pane. The volume information page displays. On this page, you can edit the volume by clicking Edit Volume. In addition, for: Standard Volume Click the Select Action dropdown menu to: o [1]Remove the volume o [2]Change the mount path and mount or unmount the volume o [3]Convert the volume to a mirror volume Mirror Volume Click the Select Action dropdown menu to: o [4]Start/[5]stop mirroring o [6]Remove the volume o [7]Promote the volume to a standard (read/write) volume o [8]Change volume mount information The page displays tabs for viewing: + Summary of the volume including: o All recent and active volume [9]alarms o Volume [10]disk usage o Volume [11]details such as the general and auditing settings for the volume, ACLs and ACEs on the volume, and volume quotas and schedules + [12]Snapshots associated with the volume References 1.
