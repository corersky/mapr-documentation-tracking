desired replication factor The number of copies of a volume that should be maintained by the MapR cluster for normal operation. When the number of copies falls below the desired replication factor, but remains equal to or above the [1]minimum replication factor, re-replication occurs after the timeout specified in the cldb.fs.mark.rereplicate.sec parameter.
