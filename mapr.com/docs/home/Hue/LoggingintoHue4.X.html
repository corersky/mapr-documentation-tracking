Logging into Hue 4.X Once Hue is installed and the configuration files have been edited, direct your browser to the IP address where you installed Hue. * Open the Hue homepage: ip_address>:8888 The following screen appears: [image1.png] * Sign in with your username, enter the password mapr and click Sign in. You can find your username in the /opt/mapr/conf/daemon.conf file.
