Changing Your Password Once you log in, you can change your Hue password. To change your Hue password, perform the following steps: 1. Click on your Username at the top right of the navigation menu bar and select Edit Profile from the drop-down menu. In this example, the username is mapr. [image9.png] The Hue Users dialog box opens. 2. Enter your current password in the Current password field and enter your new password in the New Password field. Retype the password in the Password confirmation field. [image13.png] 3. Click Update user.
